About Us

LIV LOVINA HOTEL

Welcome to Hotel Liv Lovina, your perfect gateway to discover the beauty of North Bali. Nestled in the center of the region’s tourism area, our hotel is surrounded by some of the island’s most popular attractions, including the tranquil Lovina Beach, the lush Sambangan Waterfalls, and many other cultural and natural destinations.

At Hotel Liv Lovina, we strive to provide not just accommodation, but a complete experience. Our modern design blends comfort and functionality, offering well-appointed rooms, relaxing public spaces, and thoughtful facilities to meet the needs of every traveler. Whether you are here for leisure, adventure, or a family getaway, our team is dedicated to delivering warm hospitality and personalized service.

With a commitment to affordability without compromising quality, Hotel Liv Lovina ensures that guests can enjoy modern facilities, a strategic location, and an unforgettable stay in North Bali.
